Lynda.com

Amazing wealth of information on a wide variety of topics. I have used their tutorials for over a year now to help me learn new frameworks, languages, and overall just better ways of doing things.

Codecademy.com

A very basic, good starting introduction into many languages. I've done almost all of the tutorials on there, since they are pretty short. I used the site to get an overall gist of what different languages are capable of and the major differences between them

Javascript Essentials & Javascript: The Good Parts

two entry-mid level Javascript books that I have read to gain a better understanding of Javascript and how to use it. A bit more in-depth than most tutorials they go over many best practices and good and bad aspects of the language
